# Workshop slides

There are accompanying slide decks that introduce the workshop and each task. They are tailored to different events and durations, so pick the one that matches how the workshop is being delivered.

| Deck | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [Full day course](https://fusion-energy.github.io/neutronics-workshop-slides/full-day.html) | The complete workshop, covering every topic from cross sections through to CAD, depletion and shutdown dose rate. |
| [Half day university course](https://fusion-energy.github.io/neutronics-workshop-slides/half-day-university.html) | The fundamentals, with a "day in the life of a fusion neutron" introduction to Monte Carlo transport, ending in a design task. |
| [Half day conference course](https://fusion-energy.github.io/neutronics-workshop-slides/half-day-conference.html) | A condensed version suitable for a conference setting and an audience already familiar with neutronics. |

All three decks can also be reached from the [slides landing page](https://fusion-energy.github.io/neutronics-workshop-slides/index.html).

The slides are written in [Marp](https://marp.app/) flavoured Markdown and the source is in the [neutronics-workshop-slides repository](https://github.com/fusion-energy/neutronics-workshop-slides).
